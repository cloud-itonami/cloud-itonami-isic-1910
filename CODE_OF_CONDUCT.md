# Code of Conduct: cloud-itonami-isic-1910

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people, regardless of background, experience, or identity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and approaches
- Providing and receiving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal identity
- Intimidation, threats, or aggressive behavior
- Trolling or spam
- Deliberate misinformation
- Discrimination based on any personal characteristic

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards and addressing violations. Violations can be reported to jun784@gmail.com.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Appropriate action will be taken, which may include:
- Warning the individual
- Removal from the community
- Reporting to platform authorities (GitHub, etc.)

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:
- GitHub Issues and Pull Requests
- Discussion forums
- Public meetings
- Any representation of the project in public
